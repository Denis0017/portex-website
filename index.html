Our Process

  1. Requirement Gathering — Grade, volume, delivery window and documentation.
  2. Supplier Match — Shortlist producers by capacity and certification.
  3. Sampling & Testing — Coordinate samples and lab verification.
  4. Contracting — Agree terms, Incoterms and payment schedule.
  5. Logistics Execution — Book transport, packing and clearance.
  6. Delivery & Feedback — Monitor arrival and capture buyer feedback.

We document every step to reduce disputes and ensure traceability.
