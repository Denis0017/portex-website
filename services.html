Our Services

Practical services that cover the entire trade lifecycle.

Salt Trading

We trade multiple grades of salt — culinary, iodized, and industrial. Our supplier network includes coastal producers and inland refineries. We manage sampling, testing and documentation to meet buyers' specifications.

Agricultural Commodities

We handle bulk shipments of grains, oilseeds and spices. Our services include contract sourcing, quality inspection, packing and shipment scheduling to key export markets.

Logistics & Customs

End-to-end logistics: from inland transport and warehousing to ocean freight booking and customs clearance. We coordinate with trusted partners to reduce delays and demurrage risks.

Quality & Compliance

We arrange lab testing and third-party inspections. Documentation support for export compliance, phytosanitary certificates and other regulatory needs is provided.

Market Intelligence & Sourcing

Custom market reports and supplier sourcing to help buyers make data-driven purchase decisions. We advise on seasonal availability and price trends.
